Our Centre

Big Brain Tuition provides students with a specially designed space to enhance learning. Private study spaces allow for students to collaborate with each other and work outside the classroom, whilst the small class sizes allow greater attention from tutor to student. The safety of children is one of our top priorities, we have CCTV in every classroom as well as a private road to allow safe entry for students.
